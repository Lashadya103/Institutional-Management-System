About Us

Dharmaraja Institute is one of the leading institutes in Aluthgama. Our primary mission is to provide O/L and A/L classes with rigorous academic challenges.

A child-centered, nurturing environment designed to spark curiosity and inspire a lifelong love of learning. Students learn by asking questions, having conversations, and developing the skills to genuinely listen to the ideas of others.

CURRICULUM

Rooted in research and led by best teachers in leading schools, our institution provides opportunities to inspire a lifelong passion for learning.

PROGRAMS

Creating the best experience for every student, through daily opportunities to learn and explore

SCHEDULE

We work within your school’s academic calendar, provide increased focus leading up to tests, and take your schedule into account when creating lesson plans.

Services

Explore what DHARMARAJA INSTITUTE offers in order to qualify students with critical skills & knowledge.

Progress Report

Provide a report card that includes all the marks on the tests at the end of the term.

Monitor Assesments

Let know about the marks of the child individually for the tests and assessments held in the class

Monitor Attendence

Let know about the attendance of the child by providing a detailed attendence report for each student.

Notifying Class Dates

Keeping you updated about the class dates, postponed or rescheduled dates, holidays and extra class days.
